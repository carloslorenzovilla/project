The Beer Recommendation App

This is the the final product of a project that began with my senior capstone. I was tasked with building a recommendation engine that made recs based on a user's consumption history. I ended up with a back end engine written in that worked with simulated data and a Tkinter GUI. After graduation I decided to develop a front end utilizing a Flask framework and publish a web app that recommends Seattle local beers.
